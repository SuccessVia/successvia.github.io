Complaint Tracking

Are keeping track of and following-up on complaints an important part of your business?

We recently developed a complaint tracking system for a state licensing board, and we thought other organizations might also find it useful. Key features of the system include:

  • Configurable workflow for processing complaints
  • Setting due dates / reminders
  • Tracking documents requested / received
  • Activity log
  • Support process analysis (internal vs. external delays)
  • Full audit trail of all changes

If complaint tracking is important to you, we would be happy to discuss our system and give you a demo to help determine if our system would benefit your organization.
